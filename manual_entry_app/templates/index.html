User Guide

Do's

  • Log in to Backoffice before the bot starts working.
  • Ensure you are on the "Labels" page.
  • Leave the browser window open and visible.
  • Use CSV headers like "Thai" or "TH", "Spanish" or "ES".

Don'ts

  • Do NOT close the browser while the bot is running.
  • Do NOT interact with the mouse/keyboard while the bot is typing.
  • Do NOT rely on the bot for critical data without checking risks.
